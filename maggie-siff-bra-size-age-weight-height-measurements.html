Biography:

Maggie Siff was born on June 21, 1974 in The Bronx, New York City, New York, US. She attended Bryn Mawr College where she graduated with a degree in English in 1996. She then completed her MFA in Acting from NYU’s Tisch School of the Arts.

In 2004, she made her television debut in an episode of the series Third Watch. She has since appeared in other shows including Rescue Me, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Grey’s Anatomy, Nip/Tuck, and Mad Men.

From 2008 to 2013, she became known for her role as Tara Knowles in the television series Sons of Anarchy. In film, she starred in such features as Michael Clayton, Funny People, and Concussion, among others.
