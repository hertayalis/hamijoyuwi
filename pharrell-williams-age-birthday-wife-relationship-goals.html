7 Times Pharrell Williams and His Wife Served Up #RelationshipGoals

Pharrell Williams is turning 43 today! The triple-threat is celebrating his birthday in the only way we'd expect him to: in utter coolness.

The rapper, singer, and record producer is currently starring as a coach on NBC's musical competition show, The Voice, alongside fellow coaches Adam Levine, Christina Aguilera, and Blake Shelton. With already one win under his belt from last year, Pharrell is hoping to make a second win using his unabashed coolness and impressive coaching skills to influence the next generation of singers.

Unfortunately, this season will be Pharrell's last for the time being: He and Aguilera are stepping down and Alicia Keys and Miley Cyrus are taking their places in the red chairs. Yet, it seems like Pharrell is already working on a new project as he recently reunited with Justin Timberlake in the studio back in March. Aside from Pharrell's musical projects, he's also siphoning out his creative talents into fashion, as he recently became G-Star's new co-owner.
