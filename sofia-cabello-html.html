Sofia Cabello - Age, Family, Bio

Younger sister of former Fifth Harmony member Camila Cabello. In 2012, she auditioned for the FOX series X Factor. 

Before Fame

She was born in the US after the Cabello's immigrated. 

Trivia

In May 2014, she appeared in an AwesomenessTV video where Camila introduced the world to her family. 

Family Life

Her parents are Alejandro and Sinuhe Cabello. Her grandmother is named Mercedes Cabello. 

Associated With

Her sister performs alongside Dinah Jane Hansen in Fifth Harmony.
