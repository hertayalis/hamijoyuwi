Rosala Wore a Green Naked Dress on Her Greece Vacation

Rosalía has been serving some serious summer looks while on holiday in Greece.

The Motomami singer was pictured this week in a series of edgy outfits, from a navy sailor-collar babydoll dress to a red leather racing jacket and pants. But it was a desert-green naked dress that was the star of the show.

She wore the sheer number—which had hues of dusty green and burnt orange—with a pair of black leather slides. Under the dress, the Spanish reggaetón star wore a white thong and nude bra. She accessorized with a massive diamond ring, shown on her ring finger; wore glossy makeup; and left her hair in natural waves.

Rosalía shared photos of the looks of the week in a photo dump on her Instagram and included a shot of her holding a giant street gyro.

Over the weekend, the "CHICKEN TERIYAKI" singer enjoyed a sunny day on a yacht on the waters of Santorini with her boyfriend, Rauw Alejandro, for which she wore a vibrant pink bikini by TRIANGL.

"Hello, I love the tomatoes and olives here and I don't want to leave," she wrote in Greek in an Instagram post featuring photos of her boat day.

The day prior, she shared more photos of herself on the yacht with her man, showing off one of the summer's hottest fashion collaborations: a tan bikini by Fendi x SKIMS. The two-piece featured the logos of both brands. Rosalía paired the swimsuit with a beige fuzzy hat and white oversized sunglasses.
