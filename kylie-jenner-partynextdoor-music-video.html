Kylie Jenner Stars in PartyNextDoor's Music Video

Kylie Jenner stars in R&B act PartyNextDoor's music video for "Come and See Me," which premiered today on Snapchat Discover—a fitting medium for the social media queen. 

In the vid, Jenner plays PND's sultry love interest, which further hints at the two's actual relationship offscreen. Jenner and the singer are reportedly dating, since word spread of their meet-ups soon after her breakup with Tyga.

Highlights in "Come and See Me" include Jenner snuggling up to a puppy, shamelessly promoting her Lip Kit and kissing PND in the rain. Take a look at some shots below.
