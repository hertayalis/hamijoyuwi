Biography

Aaron Copland was born in Brooklyn, NY on November 14, 1900. Populist composer credited with developing the American style of composition. He was known as the Dean of American Composers. He taught himself how to conduct and would at times consult his close friend Leonard Bernstein for help in developing his technique and style. He studied first with composer Rubin Goldmark, then abroad with Isidor Philipp, Paul Vidal, and pedagogue Nadia Boulanger.
