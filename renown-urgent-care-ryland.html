Overview

Extended Hours for Your Convenience

For medical concerns that are urgent but not life-threatening, visit Renown Urgent Care – Ryland, conveniently located in the heart of Reno. Receive treatment for a wide range of minor injuries and illnesses requiring same-day medical attention, such as cuts, fevers, fractures, burns, or allergic reactions.

Appointments: Easily schedule a specific time at this or any Renown Urgent Care location.
