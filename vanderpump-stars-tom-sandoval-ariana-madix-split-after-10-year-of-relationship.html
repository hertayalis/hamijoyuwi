Vanderpump Stars Tom Sandoval &amp; Ariana Madix Split After 10 Year of Relationship

It looks like another hit reality TV romance has met its end. After Ten years together, Ariana Madix and Tom Sandoval, two of the stars of Vanderpump Rules, have gone their separate ways following reports that Sandoval was unfaithful to his longtime love with model Raquel Leviss.

Fans of the show are reeling from the news as so much time had been devoted to chronicling their relationship’s ups and downs since it began all those years ago. Read more to know the details….

Ariana Madix and Tom Sandoval, the longest-running couple of Vanderpump Rules, have reportedly broken up after Tom was allegedly caught cheating with Raquel Leviss.

According to TMZ, production has resumed on their show to capture the aftermath of their split, leaving fans wondering if it will be showcased on Pump Rules season 10.

The pair have been together since 2013 when they first appeared as a couple on the show and have often spoken about wanting to get married.

However, all hopes of walking down the aisle seem to have gone out the window after Raquel took to Instagram to post screenshots of text messages from Tom in which he discussed his relationship status with Ariana.

Despite previously denying rumors of an open relationship between them, it looks like Ariana and Tom’s story may be coming to an end as they move forward with their separate lives.

The split between Ariana Madix and Tom Sandoval is sure to ruffle some feathers in the celebrity gossip world.
