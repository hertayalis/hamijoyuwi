Ellen Fisher Net Worth

Ellen Fisher is an American social media star and advocate for natural parenting. She was born on May 13, 1987 and has since gained a large following on YouTube and Instagram, with over 490,000 subscribers and 460,000 followers respectively. She promotes breastfeeding, home birthing and vegan eating, and has become a popular voice in the natural parenting community.
