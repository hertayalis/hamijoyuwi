Jennifer Lopez Is Back at the Gym After Inauguration Performance

Following her performance at the inauguration of President Joe Biden and Vice President Kamala Harris this week, Jennifer Lopez has returned to the gym, with an extra-special bling cup in tow.

J.Lo has been spotted hitting the gym on multiple occasions in recent weeks, and fans are always excited to see the superstar's choice of activewear. From using her $100,000 Hermès Himalayan crocodile Birkin as a gym bag, to showing off an impressive array of sparkly face masks to her workouts, Lopez is a constant style inspiration.

Photographed in Miami, Florida on Thursday, the Hustlers star wore skintight black leggings with a white casual workout top. She carried her souvenir bling cup from the inauguration, emblazoned with the slogan, #inauguration2021.

Lopez is regularly seen carrying a Coach bling cup, so her latest version is a special mark of support for Biden and Harris. Made by Teneka Moore, the founder of TaylorMade Bling Embellishments, J.Lo's latest bling cup is the perfect accessory for the start of a new era for the United States.

MiamiPIXX/Vaem / BACKGRID

In an interview with Entertainment Tonight, Moore opened up about J.Lo's affinity for bling cups. "I couldn't have asked for a better blessing," Moore said. "It's been so crazy ... Ever since then, she orders a cup for every event, every promotion that she does. Any movie, anything that she does, she commemorates it with a cup. It's awesome."

Moore also revealed, "The first time she received the cup, maybe like a month later, they ordered a cup for Alex, and it's, like, an all-black monochromatic cup ... Guys can bling too!"
